Visit Siliguri

Enjoy the scenic beauty within mountains and rivers in this city situated on the foothills of the Himalayas.

Top three activities to do at Siliguri

Take a journey to the Bengal Safari.

Watch out for wild animals like Black Bear, Rhino, Tiger.

Make a quick trip to Rohini Garden

Spend some leisurely time away from the city in this beautiful lake and garden.

Visit the City Centre Mall to go shopping.

Or watch movies in theatre, play games, go clubbing etc.
